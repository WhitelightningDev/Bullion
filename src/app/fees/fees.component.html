Costs and Fees

Fees recouped from the pools are used for paying costs associated with administration, insurance, and safekeeping of assets.

Fees Overview

Stock is bought at wholesale prices, and NO margin is added. All costs are fully transparent. The fee structure below may change with thirty days' notice, and any changes will be updated on our website. All fees are subject to VAT.

Fee Structure

All fees exclude VAT unless otherwise stated.
