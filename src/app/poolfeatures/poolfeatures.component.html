Investment Pools

Explore the benefits and features of our precious metal investment pools.

How the Pools Work

Member's funds are used to buy units in pools selected by the member. The pools are invested in precious metals (95% or more) and cash or other instruments depending on the type of pool. Gold and silver stock consist of Krugerrands (1Oz and 1/10 Oz Gold Krugers and 1 Oz Silver Krugers) which are very liquid and acceptable worldwide. Gold Krugerrands do not attract VAT.

Valuation & Flexibility

The pools are valued on a daily basis and funds flowing in or out will buy or redeem units at the unit price of the day. Members are free to decide how deposits are to be allocated in the available pools.

Ownership & Control

The pools are managed and controlled by the Co-op but owned by members via units. A member may redeem their units for cash or for precious metals in the pool or a combination thereof at any given time subject to notice periods. Members may also switch between pools and transfer units to another member as payment.

Membership Requirement

To invest in any available pool, it is necessary to be a member of Bullion Beperk Co-operative Limited. All personal data, holdings and co-op information are kept strictly confidential. Member login gives access to personal data and member interest values.

Member's Options After Investing

Members may withdraw units (partially or in full) by way of CASH and/or STOCK from their respective pools at any given time subject to notice periods.

Should a member opt to withdraw (take delivery of) gold or silver, it can either be collected from our dispatch point in Pretoria or Johannesburg or be sent to the member by secure courier.
