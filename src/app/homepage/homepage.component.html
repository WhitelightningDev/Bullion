How does Bullion work?

A Smarter Way to Grow & Secure Wealth

Member funds are unitized in separate pools, similar to unit trusts, and are closed on an annual basis. Units are bought or redeemed at the daily price, with 95–99% of each pool invested in hard metals.

Pools are managed by the Co-op but owned by members via units. Members may redeem units for cash, metals, or both at year-end, or as decided by the Board.

Membership is required to invest in any pool. All data and holdings are kept strictly confidential.

Member login provides access to personal data and investment values.
